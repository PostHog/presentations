RoundHog — full font family
============================
A rounded typeface derived from Inter, in the spirit of Open Runde.

9 weights, each with an italic (18 fonts total):
  Thin, ExtraLight, Light, Regular, Medium, SemiBold, Bold, ExtraBold, Black

/desktop  — .ttf files. Select all and install together.
/web      — .woff2 files for websites.

All corners rounded; radius tapered by weight so heavier weights stay legible.

Credit: Inter by Rasmus Andersson; rounding approach after Open Runde
by Laurids Kern. Licensed under SIL OFL 1.1.
